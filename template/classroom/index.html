Student Management System

Student-Teacher

About Us

Student Management System is a student-teacher portal with various classroom features like uploading assignments, submissions, Class Notice and many more. Students and Teacher can login in the portal and can experience interactive classroom experience online. One of the most powerful features of class manager is its student managing abilities. Teacher can easily manage their students and keep them updated about all of the activities in class.

Our Services

For Teacher

  1. Teachers can enroll students into their class using the Add Student feature.
  2. Once students are added, teachers can assign or update their marks for the class.
  3. Teachers can create and send notices that will reach all students in their class.
  4. Assignments can be uploaded by teachers using the Upload Assignment option.
  5. Teachers can review submitted assignments and assign marks accordingly.
  6. Students can send messages directly to teachers, which are received in the teacher's Inbox.
  7. Teachers can view all marks given to a student through the Marks profile and make edits if necessary.
  8. Users can personalize their account by uploading a profile picture via the Edit Profile option.

For Student

  1. Students can log into the portal to stay informed about all ongoing class activities and updates.
  2. Important announcements from teachers are available in the Class Notice section.
  3. Students can download assignments from the Assignments tab and upload their completed work there. (Note: Once submitted, assignments cannot be modified.)
  4. Through the All Teachers List, students can send messages to any teacher for queries or guidance.
  5. Marks and performance details can be viewed under the Marks section.
  6. Students can personalize their account by uploading a profile picture in the Edit Profile section.
